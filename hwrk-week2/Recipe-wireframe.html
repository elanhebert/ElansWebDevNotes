Best Easy Banana Bread!

Intro

This recipe is my favorite because its super consistent, doesn't take a lot of time, and can all be made in one bowl!

Ingredient List

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 super ripe bananas
  • 1 egg
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla
  • optional spices
    • cinnamon
    • nutmeg

Instructions

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. In a bowl mash bananas well, then stir in melted butter
  3. Add the remaining ingredients to the bowl, adding flour last and making sure not to overmix! (overmixed flour will result in tough bread)
  4. Add the batter to a buttered loaf pan, and (optional) sprinkle some white sugar overtop to create a pretty and yummy shell!
  5. Bake banana bread for 55-65 minutes, or till a knife inserted comes out clean
  6. enjoy!!
